Over 2 billion people can't afford the glasses they need.

Optical manufacturing has always been an esoteric process, limited to select corporations and trained lenscrafters. Now, it's trending towards higher prices and lower affordability.

We want to change that.

Team CONTACT is investigating a different way to make lenses, as the need for them grows and their price of manufacture continues to increase. 3D printing is an emerging technology that allows for the manufacture of countless items to a high standard, using incredibly low-cost materials. Our goal is to harness its benefits to enter the world of lens manufacture, allowing for individuals across continents and social classes to access the art of lenscrafting like never before.
